# Billboard Promo — Phase 7 Plan

## Phase 7: Close — End with confidence

### Goal
Finish the video with a calm, premium, confidence-building close.

### Core idea
The ending should feel clear, memorable, and polished. The viewer should leave with a simple takeaway and a strong brand impression.

### Visual concept
Return to a quiet black void. Reveal the Avenir brand lockup, the website, and one final line that summarizes the promise.

### What should appear on screen
Use:
- the Avenir wordmark or logo
- the website URL
- one closing line such as “Pay once. Stay visible forever.”

### Motion language
Keep the ending spacious and confident.
Use:
- a clean reveal
- subtle glow or soft brand accent
- a calm hold on the final line

The close should feel earned, not overstated.

### Timing
Suggested timing:
- 0:27.5–0:35

This should be the final emotional landing of the video.

### Design notes
- Keep the frame uncluttered
- Let the brand and the line breathe
- Use the orange accent sparingly

### Typography and UI cues
- Use the brand lockup cleanly
- Keep the URL small and readable
- Use the final line in a larger, more prominent style

### Motion notes for implementation
Use a simple ending rhythm:
1. Black void holds for a beat
2. Brand lockup appears
3. Final line enters
4. URL appears underneath
5. Hold and fade gracefully

### What this phase must achieve
By the end of this phase, the viewer should feel:
- the message is clear
- the product feels real and premium
- the brand feels confident

### Implementation guidance for Cursor
Treat this as a calm brand close, not a loud ending.
The strength should come from clarity and confidence.

### Cursor prompt
Create the closing scene for the Billboard promo. Return to a quiet black void and reveal the Avenir wordmark, the website URL, and one final line: Pay once. Stay visible forever. Keep the ending calm, polished, and confident, with just enough glow and spacing to feel premium.

### Success criteria
- The ending feels premium and memorable
- The brand lockup is clear
- The final line is easy to remember
- The video closes with confidence
